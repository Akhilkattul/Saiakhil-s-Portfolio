Car Rental System

The Car Rental Management System is a comprehensive application designed to streamline the car rental process for both customers and rental companies. This system leverages Python and SQL to provide a robust and user-friendly platform that efficiently handles all aspects of car rentals, from customer registration to car reservations and returns.

  1. Customer Registration and Login:
    • Users can create accounts with personal details and login securely.
    • Profile management options for updating personal information.
  2. Car Listings:
    • Displays a list of available cars with details such as make, model, rental price, and availability status.
    • Users can search for cars based on various criteria like type, price range, and availability dates.
  3. Booking and Reservations:
    • Integrates secure payment gateways for processing rental fees.
  4. Admin Dashboard:
    • Admins can manage car listings, including adding new cars, updating details, and removing cars from the inventory.
    • User management features to monitor and assist customers.

Technologies Used

  • Python: The core programming language for building the application, ensuring a robust backend.
  • SQL:Used for database management, storing user data, car details, and transaction records.
  • Visual Studio Code:Development environments used for coding and testing the application.
The Car Rental Management System aims to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of car rental services by providing an automated solution that minimizes manual intervention and errors.

Design & Fabrication Drill jig to Drill inclined holes

The project titled "Design and Fabrication of Drill Jig to Drill Inclined Holes" was undertaken as a part of the final year engineering curriculum. The primary objective of this project was to enhance productivity and maintain part accuracy in mass production by developing a specialized tool—a drill jig—that enables precise drilling of inclined holes with minimal manual intervention.

Objective:
In mass production, achieving high productivity and maintaining part accuracy are critical. This project aimed to design a drill jig that can
  1. Facilitate the drilling of inclined holes with high precision.
  2. Reduce manual intervention in the drilling process.
  3. Improve the location accuracy of the part being drilled.
  4. Enhance overall efficiency in the production process.
